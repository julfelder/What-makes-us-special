What Makes Wash U Different

The Accomodations

Washington Universiy boasts some of the most modern and accomodating dorms in the country along with many Reslife owned off-campus apartments. Check out the different housing options on the reslife website. Additionally, Wash U is nationally recognized for the on campus dining options. Check out the dining services website for more information.

Involved Classes

Class sizes at Wash U tend to be smaller and more intimate with a more hands on approach by the teachers. Even the larger classes are supplimented with small sub-sections taught by stellar TAs.

Diverse Student Body

The student body of Washington University is incredibly diverse with representatives from all over the country and around the world. Additionally, stuents come from a wide range of backgrounds with unique stories and interests. This diversity helps contribute to the intellectual stimulation and the innovative environment of the school.
